<div></div>  <div class="acf-block article-text-block" id="block-">
    <div class="wp-block-row-rowlayout article-text">              <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Moodle is delighted to welcome Edu Tech as the first Moodle Certified Partner in Sri Lanka. This partnership reinforces Edu Tech’s leadership in delivering comprehensive, robust digital learning ecosystems across the South Asia region. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“Achieving Moodle Certified Partner status is a landmark moment in our decade-long journey,” said Sanjaya Ratnayake, CEO of Edu Tech. “This certification validates our commitment to excellence and recognises our team’s expertise in delivering secure, scalable Moodle solutions that meet the rigorous standards set by Moodle HQ. We are proud to be recognised as a trusted technical authority in the region.”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Edu Tech’s certification comes at a time when Sri Lanka is experiencing rapid growth in digital learning adoption, driven by a large youth population, rising demand for quality education, and strong government focus on nationwide digital education frameworks. </span></p>

Their capabilities also include strategic and technical consulting, Sinhala and English courseware development, and interactive digital content creation, supported by strong expertise in performance optimisation and learner engagement analytics. </span></p>

<li style="font-weight: 400;"><b>Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing (SLIM) </b><span style="font-weight: 400;">– Maintaining academic integrity through a Turnitin integration, supported by custom dashboards and performance reporting.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><b>Asia Asset Finance PLC </b><span style="font-weight: 400;">– Implementing a comprehensive corporate training LMS that automates user synchronisation and tracks employee development in real time.</span></li>
