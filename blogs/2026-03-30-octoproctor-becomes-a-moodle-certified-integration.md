<div></div>  <div class="acf-block article-text-block" id="block-">
    <div class="wp-block-row-rowlayout article-text">              <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As remote and hybrid assessments become the norm, institutions are under increasing pressure to deliver secure, scalable, and trustworthy online exams without creating a more complex environment for learners or educators.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This is exactly the kind of situation OctoProctor was developed to help solve. With a browser-based, privacy-first approach to online proctoring, OctoProctor helps organisations protect exam integrity while maintaining accessibility and a genuinely intuitive user experience.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Now, we’re delighted to announce that OctoProctor has become a Moodle Certified Integration. Joining the programme is a key milestone because it ensures a fully native, reliable proctoring experience aligned with Moodle’s standards. In other words, it gives institutions confidence that exam integrity can be strengthened without disrupting the familiar Moodle experience.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“As an engineer, I’ve always valued open standards, stable integrations, and systems that work reliably at scale. So becoming a Moodle Certified Integration is a natural step for OctoProctor. Moodle’s open-source foundation reflects values we deeply share, and we are aligned in our mission to expand access to education through flexible, secure, and customisable technology,” said Anton Skshidlevsky OctoProctor CTO and Founder.</span></p>

  <div class="acf-block article-text-block" id="block-SG93IGRv">
    <div class="wp-block-row-rowlayout article-text">              <h2>How does OctoProctor work?</h2>
              <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">OctoProctor integrates directly with Moodle, allowing organisations to launch and manage secure, proctored exams within their existing LMS environments. There’s no need for additional platforms, downloads, or complex setup — everything runs directly in the browser.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">When an exam begins, OctoProctor performs identity verification, environment checks, and continuous monitoring throughout the session. AI-assisted detection and flexible proctoring modes (automated, live, or hybrid) help ensure integrity while adapting to different assessment needs.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Educators and administrators gain access to clear, audit-ready insights, including:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Full session timelines and recordings</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Structured incident reports and flags</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Evidence for review, appeals, and compliance</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The result is a transparent, defensible assessment process that supports both institutional requirements and student trust.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Being officially certified as a Moodle Certified Integration means that OctoProctor meets rigorous standards for performance, security, and user experience. Students can access exams instantly without installing plugins or additional software, reducing technical barriers and support overhead. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At the same time, built-in protections — for example, identity verification, AI monitoring, and browser-level controls — help safeguard exam integrity. </span></p>
