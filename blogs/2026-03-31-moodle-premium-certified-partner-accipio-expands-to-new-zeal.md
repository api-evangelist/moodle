<div></div>  <div class="acf-block article-text-block" id="block-">
    <div class="wp-block-row-rowlayout article-text">              <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’re excited to announce that Accipio, a Moodle Premium Certified Partner in the UK since 2021, has expanded into New Zealand. This milestone reinforces Accipio’s position as a global leader in delivering complex, enterprise-grade digital learning ecosystems and brings its expertise to organisations across New Zealand.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“Becoming a Moodle Certified Partner in New Zealand is both a strategic milestone and a personal one for Accipio,” said Sascha Benson-Cooper, CEO and Founder of Accipio. “As a Kiwi-founded organisation, returning to New Zealand feels like a homecoming — but we arrive with significant international experience and scale behind us.”</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The expansion aligns with a period of rapid evolution in New Zealand’s learning sector, where organisations across education, government, and industry are shifting towards adaptable and scalable digital learning platforms that connect easily with other systems. This means there are growing opportunities to enhance the learner experience and automate more complex workflows to create a fully connected and engaging learning platform.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">“Being a Moodle Certified Partner in New Zealand as well as the UK means we can bring a more advanced, enterprise-led approach to the market,” added Sascha. “There is strong potential for Moodle Workplace here, particularly where organisations are ready to move beyond basic LMS usage and adopt it as a strategic workforce learning platform.”</span></p>

    <div class="wp-block-row-rowlayout article-text">              <p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Accipio delivers full lifecycle Moodle LMS and Moodle Workplace services, including architecture design, implementation, systems integration, optimisation, and ongoing managed support. The company has deep experience in complex environments, including multi-tenant deployments, regulated training programmes, and large-scale workforce learning systems.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Central to its offering is </span><a href="https://moodle.com/partners/accipio-new-zealand/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Accipio One</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, a proprietary extension framework that enhances Moodle solutions with integrated eCommerce, workflow automation, compliance and apprenticeship tools, enterprise system integration, and advanced analytics. Supported by a value-led delivery model — including continuous support, free upgrades, and unrestricted platform access — Accipio helps organisations turn Moodle LMS and Moodle Workplace into fully scalable learning and performance platforms.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">With </span><a href="https://moodle.com/news/moodlemoot-global-25-sascha-benson-cooper/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Accipio’s global expertise</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and Moodle’s open-source foundation, organisations in New Zealand gain access to flexible, future-ready digital learning solutions.</span></p>
